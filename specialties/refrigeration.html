Industrial Refrigeration

Overview

Indus Automation delivers robust refrigeration automation solutions where safety, precision, and uptime are critical.

Systems We Support

  • ▶ Ammonia pumps — safety interlocks, pressure monitoring
  • ▶ Blast chillers — temperature sequencing, fan/compressor coordination
  • ▶ Condensers — fan/pump control, pressure-based staging
  • ▶ Coolers — temperature regulation, airflow control
  • ▶ Arena ice — brine system control, pump coordination
  • ▶ Compressors — VFD control, load management, staging
  • ▶ Freezers — precise temperature control, alarm handling
  • ▶ Brine pumps — flow/pressure monitoring, interlocking
  • ▶ Recirculators — level monitoring, safety shutdown logic
  • ▶ Underfloor heating — temperature control, energy-efficient strategies
