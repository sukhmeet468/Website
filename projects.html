Featured Projects

Selected case studies showcasing our end-to-end automation capabilities across water treatment, grain handling, machine automation, and food processing.

01
Water & Wastewater Treatment

Split Lake WTP Upgrade

Situation
The Split Lake Water Treatment Plant required a complete automation upgrade to meet modern operational and regulatory standards. The existing control system was outdated and lacked remote monitoring capability.
Task
Deliver a turnkey automation solution covering control panel design, instrumentation supply, SCADA system configuration, PLC programming, and full on-site commissioning.
Action
Indus designed and fabricated custom control panels, supplied and integrated all process instrumentation, developed PLC programs and SCADA interfaces, and commissioned the entire system on-site.
Result
The plant operates with full automation, remote SCADA monitoring, improved compliance reporting, and significantly reduced manual intervention — delivering safe, reliable drinking water to the community.
02
Agriculture & Grain Handling

Automated Grain Receiving System

Situation
A grain handling facility needed to automate their receiving process, which relied on manual control of conveyors, shutters, bucket elevators, and drag conveyors — leading to slow throughput and operator fatigue.
Task
Design and implement an automatic control system to manage the entire grain receiving process — starting, stopping, and controlling direction of conveyors while managing elevator and drag conveyor speeds.
Action
Indus developed PLC-based sequencing logic with interlocking, installed VFDs for speed control on bucket elevators and drag conveyors, and integrated safety systems with clear operator interfaces.
Result
Fully automated grain receiving with faster throughput, reduced operator workload, consistent equipment protection, and safe sequenced startup/shutdown across the entire material handling chain.
03
Machine Automation

Automated Beef Tallow Coating System

Situation
A production facility required precise, automatic application of beef tallow coating to product moving on conveyors. Manual pump speed adjustment was inconsistent and caused waste and quality variation.
Task
Automate pump speed control to apply coating at the correct rate by monitoring conveyor speed and product flow in real time — eliminating manual adjustment.
Action
Indus programmed a PLC to monitor conveyor and flow sensors, then automatically adjust VFD-controlled pump speed proportionally. The system included operator interface screens for monitoring and adjustment.
Result
Consistent, even coating application with reduced material waste, improved product quality, and zero manual speed adjustment required during production runs.
04
Food Processing

Five-Line Packaging Conveyor Upgrade

Situation
A food processing plant had five packaging conveyor lines with manual box handling, frequent collisions, inconsistent label printing, and no centralized process visibility — limiting throughput and creating quality issues.
Task
Upgrade all five lines to a fully automated packaging system with coordinated box flow, collision prevention, automatic label generation, seamless motor control, and full SCADA process visualization.
Action
Indus designed new control panels, programmed PLC logic for box detection and collision avoidance, integrated automatic label printers triggered by box sensors, implemented smooth VFD motor control, and built SCADA screens depicting the entire process in real time.
Result
All five lines run fully automated with zero box collisions, accurate automatic labeling, smooth coordinated flow, and complete real-time SCADA visibility — increasing throughput and eliminating manual intervention.
