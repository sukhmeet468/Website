Pump Control Panel Solutions

Simplex, Duplex & Triplex Control Panels — CSA-Certified Solutions for Wastewater & Utility Applications

Overview

Indus Automation designs and manufactures custom pump control panels for new installations and retrofit projects. Designed for flexibility and long-term reliability, each panel can be configured to match your exact operational and environmental requirements.

From compact indoor installations to harsh outdoor environments, our panels provide safe motor control, accurate monitoring, and clear operator interfaces for uninterrupted operation and simplified maintenance.

Why Choose Our Panels

  • ▶ CSA-certified, code-compliant designs
  • ▶ Configurable for indoor or outdoor environments
  • ▶ Clear operator interfaces and reliable monitoring
  • ▶ Designed, assembled, and tested entirely in-house
  • ▶ Built to meet CSA, CSAus, or cCSAus requirements

Panel Configurations

We build panels for a range of pump control scenarios:

  • ▶ Simplex — Single-pump systems with manual or automatic control
  • ▶ Duplex — Dual-pump systems with lead/lag and alternating control
  • ▶ Triplex — Three-pump systems for higher capacity and redundancy

Standard Features

  • ▶ Steel enclosure
  • ▶ Door-mounted disconnect switches
  • ▶ Low/High level alarms
  • ▶ Pilot light alarm & status indication
  • ▶ Remote dry contact alarm & status indication
  • ▶ Float/pump control logic
  • ▶ Alarm buzzer
  • ▶ Hand-Off-Auto (HOA) switches

Additional Features Available

▶ Stainless steel NEMA 4X enclosure ▶ Additional float(s) & logic ▶ LED alarm beacon ▶ Elapsed time metering ▶ Pump cycle counting ▶ Alarm horn ▶ Control transformers ▶ Forward/reverse motor contactors ▶ Leak/temperature monitor relays ▶ Oil-sensing ▶ Receptacle ▶ Auto dialer ▶ Heated enclosure (outdoor) ▶ Thermal insulation (outdoor) ▶ Alarm test/acknowledge pushbuttons ▶ Inner swing panel (outdoor) ▶ Padlocked enclosures (outdoor)

Starter & Drive Options

  • ▶ VFD (Variable Frequency Drive)
  • ▶ Soft starter
  • ▶ Across-the-line starter

Control & Monitoring

  • ▶ Level, pressure, and flow-based control
  • ▶ Lead/lag and alternating pump control
  • ▶ Alarm handling and remote monitoring
  • ▶ PLC, HMI, and SCADA integration
